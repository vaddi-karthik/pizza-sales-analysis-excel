# 🍕 Pizza Place Sales Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

Excel-based interactive sales dashboard analyzing pizza restaurant performance using Pivot Tables, XLOOKUP, KPI cards, and data visualization.

## Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard](dashboard.png)

## Project Overview
Transformed raw CSV data from 4 relational datasets into a fully interactive Excel dashboard to analyze pizza sales performance, customer ordering patterns, and revenue trends.

## Objectives
- Analyze customer ordering behavior and revenue trends
- Track KPI metrics and business performance
- Identify top-performing pizzas and categories
- Discover peak sales hours and busiest days

## Dataset Information
- 21,350 customer orders
- $817,860 total annual revenue
- 4 relational tables — orders, order details, pizzas, pizza types
- 1 full year of transactional sales data (2015)

## Key Insights
- 🍕 Thai Chicken Pizza was the #1 seller at $43,434 in revenue
- 📅 Friday was the busiest day with 3,538 orders
- ⏰ Peak hours were 12–1 PM and 5–7 PM
- 📏 Large size pizzas contributed 46% of total revenue
- 📆 July was the best performing month at $72,558

## Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- XLOOKUP & SUMIFS
- KPI Cards
- Interactive Slicers
- Data Cleaning & Helper Columns
- Dashboard Design

## Dashboard Features
- 5 KPI Cards — Total Orders, Revenue, Avg Order Value, Best Month, Top Pizza
- Monthly Revenue Trend Line Chart
- Revenue by Category Donut Chart
- Top 10 Pizzas Bar Chart
- Orders by Hour Column Chart
- Orders by Day of Week Column Chart
- Interactive Slicers for Month and Category filtering

## Project Outcome
This project demonstrates practical skills in data cleaning, relational data modeling, Excel formulas, pivot table analysis, and business dashboard design.
